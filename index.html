About me

My name is Olivia Dewar-Sawin.I enjoy living every moment with wonder and fascination, always looking out for something interesting to do to occupy my curious mind and fun creative nature. I graduated from Curtin University with a Bachelor of Medical science degree and worked in a fast paced multi displinary laboratory for more than 10 years before I took on the full time role of motherhood to be with my two beautiful children.Since I left working in the laboratory, I took on the challenge franchisee business in abacus mathematics tuition centre for 5 years, during which I have discovered myself to be a person who connects well with others, a patient,dedicated motivator with love for mentoring children,inspiring them to love Mathematics and problem solving. My positive energy is created by the desire to dicover, learn and create something new or taking on a challenge for self improvement. My favourite moments are of travelling to new destinations overseas,nature walks and enjoying family picnic times in the park.
